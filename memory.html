Memory Game

Welcome commander, the aim of this mini memory game is to save your fellow squad members from the aliens trying to hunt them. To win you must save each squad member by matching their cards together without letting the aliens overrun you by matching ANY of their cards. The higher the difficulty the more alien cards that are included in the game. Good luck Commander.
